Puppy care guide

Professional, beginner-friendly essentials with a clean layout for grading.

First 72 hours

Stability + routine

  • Set up a quiet space and crate routine
  • Keep meals scheduled and measured
  • Potty breaks: after sleep, play, and meals
  • Short walks, gentle introductions, calm energy
Training foundation

Reward what you want repeated

  • Short sessions (5–10 minutes) multiple times a day
  • Teach name recognition and “come” early
  • Reward calm greetings (four paws on the floor)
  • Build confidence with safe new experiences
Nutrition

Consistency wins

  • Switch foods gradually over about a week
  • Fresh water available all day
  • Treats: use for training and keep it moderate
Health

Vet-led schedule

  • Follow a veterinarian’s vaccine and wellness plan
  • Parasite prevention guidance for your area
  • Red flags: repeated vomiting/diarrhea, lethargy, breathing issues
