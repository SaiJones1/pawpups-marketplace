Breed guide

Compare energy, grooming, and best-fit households.

Golden Retriever

Friendly + family focused

Often eager to please and great for households that enjoy routine walks and play.

Energy: Medium–High Grooming: Regular brushing Best for: Families
Siberian Husky

Athletic + enrichment driven

Thrives with structured exercise, mental games, and consistent training.

Energy: High Grooming: Heavy shedding Best for: Active owners
French Bulldog

Compact companion

Often affectionate and suitable for smaller spaces with moderate activity needs.

Energy: Low–Medium Grooming: Low Best for: Apartments
German Shepherd

Highly trainable

Strong working drive and loyalty when trained with consistency and purpose.

Energy: High Grooming: Moderate shedding Best for: Training-focused
Tip
Match breed energy to your weekly schedule.
More consistency beats “big weekend workouts.”
